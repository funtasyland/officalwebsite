Before You Book

Planning Tips for Parents

At Funtasy Party Studio, we specialise in delivering beautifully curated, fully hosted kids birthday parties in Gordon, Sydney. Our goal is to make the entire experience effortless for parents — from planning to the day of the celebration.

Booking & Guest Confirmation

To ensure everything runs smoothly, we require the final number of guests at least 14 days prior to your event.

Please also inform us of any dietary requirements or allergies so our team can prepare accordingly and provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all children.

Parents' Attendance

Our parties are a fully hosted, drop-off experience with trained staff supervising at all times. We recommend no more than 2 adults on-site.

A parent lounge room is available for families who wish to stay and relax while children enjoy their party in a separate, supervised space.

Optional adult catering available for the parent lounge upon request.

Payment & Cancellation

  • A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking
  • Full payment must be completed 7 days prior to the event
  • Bookings not finalised within this timeframe may be released
  • With at least 4 weeks' notice, we're happy to assist with rescheduling

Customisation Options

Our packages are thoughtfully designed to offer a complete experience, however we're always happy to personalise your celebration. You may:

  • Combine selected activities
  • Adjust party flow
  • Add additional experiences or upgrades

Our team will work with you to create a unique and memorable celebration tailored to your child.
